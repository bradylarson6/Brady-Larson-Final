Welcome to Formula 1

Formula 1 is the ultimate test of speed, precision, and engineering. With cutting-edge cars racing at over 200 mph, F1 combines driver skill with technological innovation on iconic circuits like Monaco and Silverstone.

How to Follow F1

    3. Check race highlights for key moments
  • Join fan communities online
  • Visit a Grand Prix in person
